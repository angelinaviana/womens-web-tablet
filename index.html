Women's Web

Kelsey Toporski's compilation of feminist content, compiled while a student at the
College of Literature, Science, and the Arts (LSA) at The University of Michigan

Growing up in a small, rural, conservative town, the Women's Studies class at LSA was really her first introduction to feminism and feminist thought, and she wanted to share her new found knowledge with others. This website is where a lot of feminist content could be compiled in one place, purely because people might not otherwise know about it. Her hometown may not have been forward thinking, but it did have an internet connection. This website currently contains 3 pages other than this one - click below to see a short description of each!


Literature

This page features books written by women and for women, books and essays about feminism, poems, plays, and bios of female writers.

History & Politics

This page features bios of important women in history and women currently in Congress or otherwise politically involved.

Feminist Dictionary

This page features a collection of important feminist terminology that might make reading easier. These will include formal definitions and my own understandings of the terms.


Kelsey's DISCLAIMER: "I did not know everything!! I'm only human - I'm still growing and learning and I have a limited knowledge of things, but I will do my best and work to make this page as inclusive and unbiased as possible."
